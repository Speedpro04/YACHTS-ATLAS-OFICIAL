# Design Inspiration - Yachts Atlas

This document captures the premium aesthetic for the Yachts Atlas platform.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy**: `#050B18` (Deep, rich navy blue)
- **Gold Accent**: `#C5A059` (Metallic gold for CTAs and highlights)
- **Gold Soft**: `#D4AF37` (For borders and delicate elements)
- **Text White**: `#FFFFFF` (Pure white for headlines)
- **Text Gray**: `#A0AEC0` (For secondary information)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Elegant, high-contrast Serif (e.g., **Playfair Display**, **Cinzel**, or **Prata**).
- **Body Text**: Clean, modern Sans-serif (e.g., **Inter**, **Montserrat**, or **Plus Jakarta Sans**).

### Elements
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle backgrounds with `backdrop-filter: blur()`.
- **Borders**: Thin, gold borders for cards and buttons.
- **Imagery**: High-resolution yacht photos, sunsets, and luxury textures.

## Layout Concepts
- **Spaciousness**: High margins and padding to convey luxury.
- **Hierarchy**: Large, bold headlines to grab attention.
- **Interactive**: Smooth transitions and hover effects on gold elements.
